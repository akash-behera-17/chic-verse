Our Story

The Journey of Elegance

Elegance Salon was founded in 2010 by Jessica Palmer, a passionate stylist with over 15 years of experience in the beauty industry. What began as a small three-chair salon has grown into a full-service beauty destination beloved by clients throughout the region.

Our journey has been driven by a simple philosophy: everyone deserves to feel beautiful, confident, and cared for. This belief guides everything we do, from the personalized attention we give each client to the continuous education we provide our staff.

Over the years, we've built a reputation for excellence, creativity, and welcoming service. We're proud to have received numerous industry awards and to have been featured in local and national media for our innovative approaches and exceptional results.
