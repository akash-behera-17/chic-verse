Our Story

Founded in 2003 by renowned stylist Maria Johnson, Elegance Salon began as a small but passionate venture with a commitment to exceptional hair care.

What started as a team of three has grown into a diverse group of talented professionals with a shared passion for beauty and a vision to transform the salon experience through artistry, expertise, and personalized care.

Over the years, Elegance has evolved into a haven where clients can relax, rejuvenate, and leave feeling more confident and beautiful than when they arrived. Our success stems from our unwavering dedication to ongoing education, mastering new techniques, and using only the finest products in the industry.
