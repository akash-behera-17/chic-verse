FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and policies.

How early should I arrive for my appointment?

We recommend arriving 10 minutes before your scheduled appointment time. This allows for check-in and consultation with your stylist or technician before your service begins.

What happens if I need to cancel my appointment?

We understand that plans change. We require at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations. Appointments canceled with less than 24 hours' notice may incur a cancellation fee of 50% of the service price.

Do you offer consultations before hair coloring services?

Yes, we provide complimentary consultations for all hair coloring services. This allows our colorists to assess your hair condition, discuss your goals, and determine the best approach for achieving your desired results.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital payment services like Apple Pay and Google Pay. Gift certificates are also available for purchase and redemption.

Do you sell the products used during services?

Yes, we offer a curated selection of the professional products used in our services. Our stylists and technicians can recommend specific products tailored to your hair and skin needs.

Is tipping customary, and if so, what is the appropriate amount?

Tipping is appreciated but not required. If you're satisfied with your service, a 15-20% tip is customary. You can tip in cash or add it to your credit card payment.
