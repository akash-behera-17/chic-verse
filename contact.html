Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

Do I need to book an appointment?

While we do accept walk-ins if we have availability, we strongly recommend booking an appointment in advance to ensure you get your preferred stylist at your preferred time.

What should I bring to my appointment?

We recommend bringing references (photos) of styles you like to help communicate your vision. If you're getting color, bringing photos of what you want is especially helpful.

How far in advance should I book for special events?

For special events like weddings or formal occasions, we recommend booking 2-4 weeks in advance. For wedding parties, please book at least 2-3 months ahead.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, cash, and mobile payment options like Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes! Gift cards are available in any denomination and can be purchased in-salon or by phone. They make perfect gifts for any occasion.
