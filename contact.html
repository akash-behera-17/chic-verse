FAQs

Common Questions

Find answers to frequently asked questions about appointments and our services.

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by calling us at (123) 456-7890, using our online booking form on this page, or sending us an email at info@elegancesalon.com with your preferred date, time, and service.

How far in advance should I book my appointment?

For regular services, we recommend booking 1-2 weeks in advance. For special occasions like weddings or events, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead to ensure availability. Last-minute appointments may be available – feel free to call us to check.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours' notice for cancellations. Appointments canceled with less than 24 hours' notice may incur a cancellation fee of 50% of the scheduled service price. No-shows will be charged the full service amount.

Do you offer consultations before services?

Yes, we offer complimentary consultations for most services. For complex color services or significant style changes, we recommend scheduling a consultation appointment before your service appointment.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital payment services like Apple Pay and Google Pay. Gift certificates are also available for purchase and redemption.
